Our Story

More Than Code.
We Build Community.

Bit-By-Bit Club was born from a simple idea — that students at VIT Bhopal deserve a space to build, fail, learn, and grow together. No politics, no gatekeeping. Just builders helping builders.

Mission & Vision

Why We Exist.

Our mission is to democratize tech education and innovation at VIT Bhopal. We believe every student has the potential to build something meaningful — they just need the right community, tools, and opportunities.

Our vision is a campus where every student, regardless of branch or background, has access to world-class technical knowledge and a network of peers who push them to do their best work.

FAQ

Who can participate in BitHack?▼

Any student currently enrolled at VIT Bhopal can participate, from any branch or year. You don't need to be a club member to register for BitHack.

What's the team size?▼

Teams of 2–4 members are allowed. Solo participation is not permitted. You can form a team before registration or use our Team Formation channel on Discord.

Is food provided?▼

Yes! All meals, snacks, and energy drinks during the 36-hour event are provided free of charge for all registered participants.

What should I bring?▼

Bring your laptop, chargers, any hardware you want to use, and your team spirit. Sleeping bags are optional but encouraged for the all-nighter!

How are projects judged?▼

Projects are judged on innovation, technical complexity, design quality, potential impact, and demo quality. Judges include industry professionals and VIT faculty.
